Weekly Prep Checklist

  • Pick 1 protein for the week.
  • Cook protein fully and let it cool.
  • Cook rice separately or in the slow cooker recipe.
  • Add vegetables, calcium, and multivitamin after cooling if your supplement label says to avoid heat.
  • Portion into wide-mouth Mason jars.
  • Refrigerate what will be used in 3–4 days and freeze the rest.
