🍽️ Beginner Mode

Every recipe should follow this pattern:

  1. Cook the protein separately.
  2. Cook the rice separately.
  3. Cook the vegetables separately.
  4. Mix everything together.
  5. Let the food cool.
  6. Add supplements.
  7. Portion into Mason jars.
  8. Refrigerate or freeze.
